Thank you for installing {{ .Chart.Name }}!

Your release is named {{ .Release.Name }}.

To access your backend application:

1. Get the backend pod name:
   export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "resilient-platform.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/component=backend" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")

2. Forward the port:
   kubectl --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward $POD_NAME 5001:5001

3. Visit http://127.0.0.1:5001 to access your application
   Health endpoint: http://127.0.0.1:5001/health

To check the status of all components:
   kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}"
